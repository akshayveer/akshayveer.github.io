Hydraulic arm is used in many machines to lift and lower heavy objects.When we apply force on the hydraulic piston this moves the arms of the crane, which rotate on pivot joints and this causes force on the other piston which finally causes tension in rope.This tension in rope is used to move the arms required to hold or lower the objects.

Hydraulic piston


Hydraulic piston works on Pascal's law ie., pressure at every point of liquid is same.
Hence on applying force on small cross section we can achieve high force on larger cross sections.
